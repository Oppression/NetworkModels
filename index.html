O3

Object Oriented Oppression (03) challenges social hackers to identify vulnerabilities in the social “code” of oppression.

This open source project was initiated by Siobhán K Cronin, Ray Gilstrap, and Pia Zaragoza during Yerba Buena Center for Arts' innaugral fellowship program, and models a relative oppression structure based on annual salary data taken from the 2012 Economic Census of the US Bureau of Labor Statistics.

The size of the circle reflects its relative oppression (the bigger the circle, the greater the oppression)

Arrows reflect the direction of relative oppression.

Double click to fade out all but category's immediate neighbours. Double-click to bring them back again.

How would you break this model?
