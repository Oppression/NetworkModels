Object Oriented Oppression (O3)

Object Oriented Oppression (O3) reveals the conceptual clustering of socio-political categorization, and challenges social hackers to identify possibilities for breaking the social “code” of oppression.

This project is a collaboration between Siobhán K Cronin, Ray Gilstrap, and Pia Zargoza, and was inspired by Yerba Buena Center for Arts' civic prompt, "Can we design freedom?"
